OPPD Residential Service Charges

Lets take a look at how OPPD has raised service fees over the last few years.

Year Service Charge Yearly Charge
2015 $10.25 $123.00
2016 $15.00 $180.00
2017 $20.00 $240.00
2018 $25.00 $300.00

Adding $5 a month, $60 year for 3 years when they already had much higher service fees than MUD does for their services, which are more maintenance intensive systems.

Residential Service Charges

2018 Rate

Rate 110 – Residential Service

This rate applies to all residential dwellings.

Service Charge: $25.00/month

Summer (June 1 – Sept. 30)

  • 10.06 cents/kWh for all kWh
  • For kWh consumption more than 100 kWh and less than 401 kWh, a credit of $2.07 per month will be applied

Winter (Oct. 1 – May 31)

  • 9.29 cents/kWh for the first 100 kWh
  • 8.03 cents/kWh for the next 900 kWh
  • 5.67 cents/kWh for all additional kWh

Minimum Monthly Bill: $27.07

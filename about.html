About Us

Clear Solutions was started in 2006, with a vision to bring professional IT support to small businesses without full-time IT staff. We recognized that the small business market is poorly served and often suffers from bad advice given by under qualified technicians. We want to form long-term partnerships with our clients to help their technology keep pace with their businesses. We want to be trusted advisor’s that our clients can count on. One thing that has always set our company apart is our ability to communicate effectively with our clients so that they can make educated decisions about their technology assets. Our company prides itself on square and honest dealing with our customers – going above and beyond to deliver results that keep our clients happy, and bring us repeat business.
